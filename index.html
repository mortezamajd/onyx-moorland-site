Professional consulting capabilities

Hydraulic, sediment transport, scour, and infrastructure resilience support.

Onyx Moorland Environmental Engineering provides specialized consulting services in hydraulic engineering, sediment transport analysis, scour evaluation, environmental engineering, and infrastructure resilience. The firm supports public agencies, engineering consultants, and infrastructure stakeholders through technically defensible modeling, engineering analysis, and regulatory-support studies.

Technical capability areas

Hydraulic and Hydrologic (H&H) Modeling

Hydrologic and hydraulic modeling support for flow, floodplain, conveyance, drainage, and infrastructure evaluation.

Sediment Transport and Morphodynamic Analysis

Assessment of sediment mobility, deposition, erosion, channel response, and long-term morphologic trends.

Scour Analysis for Bridges and Levees

Evaluation of general scour, local scour, pier scour, abutment scour, and levee/channel protection adequacy.

FEMA and USACE Support Studies

Technical support for levee certification, authorized channel capacity, regulatory review, and planning-level documentation.

HEC-RAS 1D/2D Modeling

One- and two-dimensional hydraulic modeling for riverine systems, flood control channels, levees, and infrastructure interfaces.

Quasi-Unsteady Sediment Modeling

Long-term sediment simulation workflows using sediment hydrographs and time-varying hydraulic conditions.

Flood Risk and Conveyance Evaluation

Evaluation of flood risk, hydraulic capacity, backwater effects, and conveyance performance under design and planning scenarios.

Environmental Permitting and Technical Studies

Support for environmental documentation, permitting context, technical review, and engineering decision support.

River and Levee System Assessment

Assessment of river, levee, and flood control system performance, vulnerability, and resilience considerations.

GIS and Engineering Data Analysis

Use of spatial datasets, engineering records, mapping, and geospatial analysis for technical screening and reporting.
